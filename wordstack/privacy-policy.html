Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 17, 2024

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our application ("WordStack").

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal or non-anonymous user data. The only information collected is:

  • Analytics Data: Includes anonymous usage statistics such as feature usage and session duration to improve the App's performance.
  • Error Report Data: Collected automatically when the App encounters a crash or error, to diagnose and resolve issues.

All data collected is anonymous and does not identify individual users.

2. How Information is Used

The data collected is used solely to:

  • Improve the App's functionality and user experience.

We do not sell, share, or disclose this data to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Security

All data is securely stored using iCloud CloudKit services. CloudKit adheres to strict security protocols to safeguard data against unauthorized access.

However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of data transmissions or storage.

4. Third-Party Services

This App may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics (e.g., Google Firebase).

These third parties may process anonymous data according to their privacy policies.

5. In-App Purchases

The App offers full access features through in-app purchases. Payment and billing information are handled directly by the app store platform (e.g., Apple App Store) and are subject to their terms and privacy policies.
