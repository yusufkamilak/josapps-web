Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 20, 2024

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our application ("Leo: Yearly Goals & Retro").

1. Information We Collect

Leo: Yearly Goals & Retro is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data outside of your device.

All data you enter in the App, including:

  • Yearly goals and objectives
  • Retrospective notes and reflections
  • Progress tracking data
  • Personal notes and entries
  • Achievement records

is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. This data is never transmitted to external servers or third parties.

2. How Information is Used

The data you enter is used solely to:

  • Track your yearly goals and retrospectives.
  • Monitor progress towards your objectives.
  • Send you local notifications and reminders.
  • Display information in home screen and lock screen widgets.

All processing happens locally on your device.

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is securely stored on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. The App uses an App Group container to share data between the main app and widget extensions, which is a standard Apple security practice.

Your data is protected by your device's security measures (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID). We do not have access to your data.

4. Data Sharing

Leo: Yearly Goals & Retro does not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties. Your data never leaves your device.

5. Third-Party Services

This App does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. We do not integrate with external services that collect user data.

6. Notifications

The App uses local notifications to remind you about:

  • Goal deadlines and milestones
  • Scheduled retrospective sessions
  • Progress check-ins
  • Year-end review reminders

These notifications are generated locally on your device and do not involve any external servers.

7. Widgets

Leo: Yearly Goals & Retro provides home screen and lock screen widgets. These widgets read data from the same local storage used by the main app. No data is transmitted externally for widget functionality.

8. Children's Privacy

Leo: Yearly Goals & Retro does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The App is suitable for all ages and does not require any personal information.

9. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
